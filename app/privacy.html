Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. As such, below is our privacy policy.

If you have any questions, would like to know what data we have about you, or would like to have us delete any data we have about you, please contact us at: info@sapphireos.com

If you've signed up for e-mail updates through our website: We collect your name and e-mail address so we can contact you about news and product announcements related to Chromatron and Sapphire Open Systems. This data is stored and processed by Mailchimp (www.mailchimp.com), as well as on our own servers. You can unsubscribe at any time, and your data will be deleted.

If you've purchased a product from us: In addition to collecting your e-mail for the purposes stated above, we also collect your mailing address and phone number so we can ship your orders to you. This data is stored on our own servers, as well as Stamps.com (www.stamps.com) and Tindie (www.tindie.com). We will delete this data from all stored locations at your request.

Additionally, if you purchased a product from us on Kickstarter, your name, e-mail address, phone number, and mailing information will also be stored on Kickstarter's servers (www.kickstarter.com).

We will only share your data with a 3rd party for the following reasons: 1) To faciliate the marketing and shipping activities described above. 2) To comply with lawful demands from government entities.

We do not share or sell your data for any other purpose.
